Autobiography

I was born in San Juan City, Philippines, and grew up in Cabuyao, Laguna. My passion for technology drove me to pursue a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science at the University of the Philippines Los Baños.

I built a modular processing system that integrated OpenCV and PaddleOCR for image processing and text extraction, ensuring data privacy for sensitive academic records.
